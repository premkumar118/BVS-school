Since Year 1986

We are Future

Supham, established in 1987, provides quality primary education in Kannada. Celebrating its silver jubilee, it introduced English and Kannada Medium High Schools in 2009. With over 130 dedicated members, Supham emphasizes all-round child development. Notable achievements include Kumari Dhanushree S.P. setting a new record with 623 marks. Supham is known for holistic education, shaping future citizens.
