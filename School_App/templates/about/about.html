About Us

My life is My Message -said by Mahatma Gandhi

Supham, established in 1987, represents a beacon of educational excellence and community service. Founded by the visionary Chairman Shri D.R. Siddappa and the dynamic Founder Secretary Shri Ravi Bhargava, the institution's journey unfolds as a compelling narrative of dedication to quality education in the Kannada medium. Senior Gandhian retired engineer Shri L.S. played a pivotal role, guiding Supham under the watchful eyes of Vaishyas, office bearers, and elders. The celebration of the silver festival marked a significant milestone, symbolized by the grand inauguration of the "Rajata Mahotsava Bhavan," a testament to Supham's impact on the city and taluk. Adapting to changing educational dynamics, Supham expanded its offerings in 2009, introducing both English and Kannada medium high schools. This strategic move showcased Supham's commitment to providing diverse educational pathways while making substantial contributions to high school education in the region. Central to Supham's success is its dedicated community of over 130 lifetime members, representing diverse walks of life. Their selfless service, expressed through time, effort, and financial contributions, has been instrumental in shaping Supham's identity as an institution that goes beyond academic achievements. Kumari Dhanushree S.P.'s exceptional academic accomplishment, scoring 623 marks, stands as a testament to Supham's commitment to excellence. Her success exemplifies the institution's core belief that education should transcend mere grades, emphasizing the empowerment of students with knowledge and skills beyond traditional academic boundaries. Supham's teaching staff, distinguished by their skill and creativity, embody the institution's child-centered approach to education. The emphasis on holistic development aligns with the philosophy that success is a continuous journey, not a destination. This ethos underscores Supham's ongoing efforts to mold future citizens who are not only academically proficient but also socially responsible. Under the leadership of Dr. H.P. Raj Kumar, serving as the Secretary, Supham continues to blaze trails in education. The institution's proactive approach to extracurricular activities underscores its commitment to providing a comprehensive educational experience. Supham places a premium on nurturing knowledgeable individuals, recognizing that true success lies in the holistic development of its students. In essence, Supham emerges as a transformative force in education, seamlessly integrating academic excellence with community service and holistic development. The founders, members, and leadership have collectively shaped an institution that not only imparts knowledge but also instills values, contributing significantly to the creation of a better future for its students and society at large.

- Dr. H.P. Raj Kumar (Secretary)
